## ðŸ“ž Call Actions

### Answering Calls

| Method | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Click | Click Answer button |
| Double-click | Double-click call |
| Keyboard | Press Enter on selected call |

### Transferring Calls

**Drag-and-Drop Transfer:**
1. Click and hold active call
2. Drag to destination extension
3. Release to transfer

**Blind Transfer:** Drops immediately
**Attended Transfer:** Click extension to announce first

### Parking Calls

1. Drag call to Parking panel
2. Or click Park button
3. Call parks in first available slot
